{% trans "About Arcade" %}

{% trans "Who was involved?" %}

Arcade is the result of a partnership between the City of Lincoln Council and the Getty Conservation Institute, with the assistance of Historic England. It is powered by Arches, an open source heritage data management platform developed by the Getty Conservation Institute and the World Monuments Fund.
